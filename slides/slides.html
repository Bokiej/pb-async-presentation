It's impossible to predict the sequence

When you query many endpoints at the same time you have to prepare to structure your results because you don't know in which order they will be returned.

Methods for many Promises

We can handle many Promises at the same time with below methods:

  • Promise.all()
  • Promise.allSettled()
  • Promise.race()
  • Promise.any()

Every method will be described in next sections.

Checkout how works bulb-app LWC component to see how difficult is to predict order of Promises.
